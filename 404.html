Mikus Drive

by Mykhailo Mikus

Browse multiple Google Drive accounts in a Windows-style file manager.

Google shows “This app hasn’t been verified”?
  1. Click Sign in with Google above.
  2. On Google’s screen, open Advanced (bottom-left).
  3. Choose Go to Mikus Drive (unsafe) — the app is open source and only accesses Drive after you approve it. Google shows this until full app review is complete.
  4. Review the permissions and click Allow.

Sign in once and allow Google Drive access when prompted.
